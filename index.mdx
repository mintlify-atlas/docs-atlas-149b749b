OwlMetry is a self-hosted observability platform designed for the agentic development era. Point your coding agent at the setup instructions and it handles everything — integration, monitoring, debugging, and performance analysis.

<Warning>
  **Alpha Software** — OwlMetry is in early alpha. APIs, schemas, and configuration may change without notice. Not yet production-ready.
</Warning>
